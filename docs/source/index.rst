Welcome to Grimlocke's Covenant!
===================================

**Grimlocke** is a secondlife roleplay community located in modern day Oregon U.S.A. It neighbors Astoria and is nestled along the river surrounded by a forrest.
Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including
how to :ref:`installation` the project.

.. note::

   The Covenant is subject to change at any time.
